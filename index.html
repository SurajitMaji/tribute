A Tribute to Someone Special

Words from a Heart That Watches from Afar

Dedication

This tribute is for someone whose
smile makes days brighter,
whose presence makes moments
meaningful, and whose youl qui-
tely inspires hearts around them.

You may never know who I am.
But I hope these words show you
what you truly deserve to hear-
that you are admired. respected,
and appreciated.

Happy Birthday

Unknown But Known

I do not know your favorite color.
Yet I see the way you light up a room.
I do not know your story.
But I see the strength you cary in silence.

You speak in ways you don’t realize-
In kindness, in grace, in quiet courage.
You move through life like poetry.
And I’ve been reading your presence like a verse.

So here’s a tribute,
From someone who noticed,
Someone who smiles silently,
Because you exist.

10 Quiet Reasons You Inspire Me

  1. Your smile feels like moming light.
  2. You treat people gently---that’s strength, not softness,
  3. You don’t need noise to be noticed.
  4. Your calmness in chaos is art.
  5. You carry digniry like a crown.
  6. You make others feel seen.
  7. Your laughter stays even affier you’re gone.
  8. You move with purpose, not pressure.
  9. You make silence feel safe.
  10. You make this worid a bit more beautiful.

A Final Note

If you ever wonder whether someone
thought of you... this mull
answer.

You may not know who I am,
but this small tribute comes from a
place of deep respect.

Heart's Eyes

- M.U.P

After the good use of unnecessary things,
the bad use of indispensable things.

In an attempt to make the traitor trustworthy,
the believer became a traitor.

In changing the rejecter into the accepted,
the accepted became a rejecter.

When the worthless finds a worthy place,
the priceless loses its grace.

In proving the deceiver true,
the truthful lost their value too.

While turning the outcast into a guest,
the welcomed became the unblessed.

Hoping the distant would start to care,
the caring faded in despair.

As the trivial gained attention bright,
the essential slipped out of sight.

In trusting the unfaithful soul,
the loyal one lost control.

While lifting the fallen to belong,
the rooted ones lost where they belong.

Wishing the absent would grow near,
the present ones disappeared in fear.

While giving the useless a new role,
the irreplaceable lost its soul.

As the ignored found a way inside,
the cherished ones were set aside.

Hoping the cold would learn to feel,
the warm hearts were left to heal.

In the hope of making the careless into the caring,
the caring remained neglected.

All in One Second

- Shorted

Nothing ever flowed with ease,
Every moment came with weight—
With you, it’s never been just time,
It’s effort shaped by fate.

I only tried to build a space,
Where your heart feels light and free,
A place where silence smiles with you,
And your soul can simply be.

Maybe I misunderstood your view,
Maybe you feel less or more—
Maybe your past holds shadows
That your present can’t ignore.

Maybe it’s not like others told you,
Maybe it’s not yet even begun—
But what’s inside your quiet mind
Feels like a rising sun.

And if the world never hears it all,
It’s okay—they don’t need to see.
Because you and I, in our silence,
Already know what it could be.

If your heart rewinds that hidden reel
Of days we never named—
You’ll find a second, just one glimpse,
Where everything almost changed.

Just one moment in your memory,
That flashes like a spark—
But even if it’s just a second,
It still lit up my dark.
